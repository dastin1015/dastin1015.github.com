About

Mar 25th, 2013

I am 19 years old. I was born in Yokosuka-shi, Kanagawa-ken, Japan. I am half Japanese and half white. I am a Coronado High School 2011 alum and was a sophomore at the University of California: Santa Cruz, now attending Southwestern Community College. I’m majoring in Computer Science. I’m very friendly and I like to make new friends, although I have a strong disliking for rude people. If you want to get to know me better feel free to talk to me and ask. I am also a device maintainer for the Samsung Epic 4G Touch and the HTC Evo 4G LTE for the Evervolv project. I am also a Recognized Developer/Contributor at XDA-Developers and go by the username dastin1015.
